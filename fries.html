French Fries

>

Description

French Fries are crispy and delicious potato snacks loved by people of all ages. They are easy to make at home and pair well with burgers, sandwiches, or enjoyed on their own.

Ingredients

  • 4 large potatoes, peeled and cut into fries
  • Vegetable oil for frying
  • Salt to taste

Steps

  1. Soak the potato fries in cold water for about 30 minutes to remove excess starch. Drain well and pat dry with a kitchen towel.
  2. Heat vegetable oil in a deep fryer or a large pot to 350°F (175°C).
  3. Carefully add the potato fries to the hot oil in batches, ensuring they are not overcrowded. Fry for about 5-7 minutes, or until they are golden brown and crispy.
  4. Remove the fries from the oil using a slotted spoon and drain on paper towels to remove excess oil.
  5. Season the fries with salt while they are still hot. Serve immediately.
