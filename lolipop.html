Chicken Lollipop

>

Description

Chicken Lollipop is a popular appetizer made from chicken wings, which are frenched, marinated, and deep-fried until crispy.

Ingredients

  • 8 chicken wings, tips removed and drumettes frenched
  • 2 tablespoons soy sauce
  • 1 tablespoon ginger-garlic paste
  • 1 tablespoon red chili powder
  • 1 teaspoon garam masala
  • 1 teaspoon black pepper powder
  • 1/4 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1/4 cup cornstarch
  • 1 egg, beaten
  • 1 cup bread crumbs
  • Oil for deep frying
  • Salt to taste

Steps

  1. In a large bowl, mix soy sauce, ginger-garlic paste, red chili powder, garam masala, black pepper powder, and salt. Add chicken wings and marinate for at least 1 hour.
  2. In a shallow dish, mix all-purpose flour and cornstarch. Dip each marinated chicken wing in beaten egg, then coat with the flour mixture.
  3. Heat oil in a deep pan over medium heat. Fry the coated chicken wings in batches until they are golden brown and crispy, about 5-7 minutes per batch.
  4. Remove the chicken lollipops from the oil and drain on paper towels to remove excess oil.
  5. Serve hot with your favorite dipping sauce.
