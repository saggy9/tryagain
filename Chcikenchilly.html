Chicken Chilly

Description

Chicken Chilly is a spicy and flavorful dish made with marinated chicken pieces stir-fried with bell peppers, onions, and a blend of spices.

Ingredients

  • 500 grams chicken, cut into small pieces
  • 2 bell peppers (capsicum), thinly sliced
  • 1 large onion, thinly sliced
  • 4 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1-inch ginger, grated
  • 2 green chilies, slit
  • 2 tablespoons soy sauce
  • 1 tablespoon chili sauce
  • 1 tablespoon tomato ketchup
  • 1 teaspoon vinegar
  • 1 teaspoon red chili powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon black pepper powder
  • 1 teaspoon cornstarch
  • 1/4 cup water
  • 2 tablespoons vegetable oil
  • Salt to taste
  • Fresh cilantro leaves for garnish

Steps

  1. In a bowl, marinate the chicken pieces with soy sauce, chili sauce, tomato ketchup, vinegar, red chili powder, black pepper powder, and salt. Let it marinate for at least 30 minutes.
  2. In a small bowl, mix cornstarch with water to make a slurry.
  3. Heat vegetable oil in a pan over medium-high heat. Add minced garlic, grated ginger, and slit green chilies. Saut√© for a minute.
  4. Add thinly sliced onions and bell peppers. Stir-fry until they are slightly tender.
  5. Add the marinated chicken pieces to the pan. Cook until the chicken is cooked through.
  6. Pour the cornstarch slurry into the pan and mix well. Cook until the sauce thickens.
  7. Garnish with fresh cilantro leaves and serve hot.
